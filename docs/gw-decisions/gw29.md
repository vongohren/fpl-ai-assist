## Decision Made

### Transfers Executed
1. **Wirtz (LIV, 8.3m) OUT -> Dewsbury-Hall (EVE, 5.0m) IN**
2. **Marc Guiu (CHE, 4.1m) OUT -> Sesko (MUN, 7.3m) IN**

### Rationale
- Removes the two weakest squad members (25% fit + 0.0 form)
- Highest 5-GW EV combo within budget (54.7 pts projected)
- Sesko is a 4.7% differential for rank climbing
- D-H has FDR 1 fixture immediately (EVE vs BUR)
- No BGW31 blank exposure added
- Fixes auto-sub chain (both new players are fit and starting)

### What we gave up
- **All Liverpool coverage** during their 2nd-best fixture run. This is the biggest sacrifice. If Gravenberch sell price had given 0.4m more, we would have gone Gravenberch + Sesko to maintain LIV.
- 0.5m extra ITB (Gravenberch would have cost 0.5m more)

### Captain decision
- **Haaland** if confirmed fit (MCI vs NFO, FDR 2)
- **Palmer** as fallback if Haaland remains doubtful at deadline

### Chip strategy
- All chips held
- Free Hit -> BGW31 (4 players blank: Gabriel, Timber, O'Reilly, Haaland)
- Wildcard -> pre-DGW (likely GW33-35 range)
- BB + TC -> DGW deployment
